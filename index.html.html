Why We Are Best

Huge School Campus

The diversification and expansion of the school through adoption of new and topically relevant courses has led to further enhancement of our infrastructure in order to fulfil our growing needs.

Science Laboratory

Our school has a well equiped laboratory of Physics,Chemistry,Biology,Computer science,Math & Social studies for the student to experiment & learn because learning by doing makes concrete concept formation.

Computer Education

Computer have taken over a major part of human activities and enabled people to have more leisure by reducing his work-load.Computer Education is started from the primary classes and is one of the optional subjects in the Senior Secondary classes.

Music & Dance

Training of Vocal as well as instrumental music is provided according to the taste and interest of the students from Std.Our cultural dance or western dance are taught in the school by experts to train,guide and help those students inerested in this form of act.







OUR COLLEGE DETAILS

College Functions

Our primary function of a Colllege is to socialize children. This means that, in the society of their peers, children learn acceptable behavior, reciprocal relationships,& appropriate conflict resolution.

College Sports

In our College which use a house system a feature of the school is the competition between the houses; this is especially brought out during sporting events such as an inter-house sports day.

College Assembly

In our College, students gather to perform a common song or prayer, and to receive common announcements with all together on our college ground.
